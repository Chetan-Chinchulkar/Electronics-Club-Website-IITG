SIGN LANGUAGE CONVERTER



A sign language glove is an electronic device which converts the complex motions of a sign language into written or spoken words. It is useful for people who canâ€™t speak.The standard sign language has already been set internationally and different gestures correspond to different alphabets and numbers. Anyone can convey their message by the simple motion of their hands and this model converts such gestures and displays on-screen. It can be further extended to spoken words.

Components used in the prototype are very usual ones:-

  • Arduino micro controller
  • capacitive flex sensor(self made)
  • capacitive touch sensor
  • gyro and accelerometer sensors oled display screen.



GENERAL DESCRIPTION



The prototype is basically a device that converts hand gesture to alpha-numeric characters.It takes input from capacitive touch and flex sensors. Micro-controller Atmega2560 is used to process the gesture signal and produce the output on the oled screen.



CAPACITIVE TOUCH SENSOR


We used aluminum foil, double-sided tape and wires to make capacitive touch sensor.The wire is connected to aluminum foil and tape is used to give strength and attach on the glove . The circuit for working of the touch sensor is shown below. When the send pin sends a square wave, it will eventually change the state of the receive pin due to discharging of the capacitor. The delay between the send pin changing and the receive pin changing is determined by an RC time constant which is calculated by the cap-sense library, delay is proportional to R * C, where R is the value of the resistor and C is the capacitance at the receive pin. When we touch the metal foil it behaves like a capacitor which changes the overall capacitance hence delay time gets changed which is detected by the cap-sense library.



CAPACITIVE FLEX SENSOR

First of all aluminum foil and paper is cut of finger size in which aluminum foil is slightly smaller in dimensions.One wire is attached to each aluminum foil.Graphene sheet is sandwiched in between the layer of aluminum foils which serve as the dielectric for the capacitor just created. To provide strength glue and tape used appropriately. The circuit for working of flex sensor is shown below. flex Bending in flex changes the dielectric constant of graphene sheet which results in change in voltage drop across the terminals of flex(capacitive). This change in voltage drop is measured by one of the analog pins of the microcontroller.

Future Aspects


further we are trying to implement it using bio-electric sensors so that we can generate the alphabets just by muscle activity.



Other Aspects


The glove is not only of the use of converting a sign language to written or spoken form, it should be rather be viewed as interacting with the computer( electronic devices ) with the help of gestures. It is like a remote fitted on your hand which controls your electronic modules as well. It can also be used for the military purpose of transferring gestures information between soldiers apart from the line of site.

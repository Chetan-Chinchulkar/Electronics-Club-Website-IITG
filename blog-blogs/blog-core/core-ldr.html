LDR Dot Matrix




In the modern world which is full of advertisement what is better than to provide the world with a cheaper and efficient way of advertisement. What is more impressive than to replace those stupid advertisement boards with a digital way of advertisement. What is better than to create a secret security system?And those fascinating automated streetlights and highway lights that can help us in saving a lot of energy every single day?

The idea:


LDR & DOT Matrix is the project that just mimics the pattern of the shadow on the LEDs matrix using signal amplification. This can also work as a prototype for a simple camera. The components used in this project includes IC 741, resistors, LDRs, LEDs etc. Few days before, we were reading a paper about working of camera and from there we got the motivation for making a simple model of a simple camera.We kept on thinking about the working of the camera which lead us to the idea of replication of an object in to a screen using its shadow detection which we accomplished using the usage of LDR matrix.

For this to work properly , we need to amplify the feeble signal obtained from the ldr and should pass it effectively to the corresponding LEDs.This was accomplished using an IC 741 (OP-amp).



Preliminaries:

  • LDR (Light Dependent Resistors)
  • LED (Light Emitting Diodes)
  • PCB (Printed Circuit Board)
  • LM IC 741(Op-Amp)
  • Resistors (180, 100k, 4.7k ohms)
  • Connecting wires
  • Power Source (9 V-12 V)
  • Light Source
  • Switch



Working

This project is based on a darkness detector circuit in which we are using IC741 to amplify the input signal which we get from LDR. When the intensity of light on LDR decreases, its resistance increases because of which input voltage to IC741 (Op-Amp) increases resulting in increase of output voltage going to LED. This smaller unit (as shown in adjoining figure, circuit diagram) is further extended to a combination of 16 such circuits by placing them parallelly. Input is taken through a 4×4 LDR matrix and sent to 4×4 LED matrix after amplification by Op-Amp.

  • Read,write and delete SMS messages
  • Send SMS messages
  • Monitor signal strength

The first one is the LDR grid which will sense the light falling that surface like a camera and transfer the signals inferred to the next block of the amplification circuit, which will amplify the signal and transfer the amplified signal to the next block that is our LED grid which is working like a black and white screen.




Applications:


  • The most important application of this project is in the ever growing advertising and designing industry. LDR LED Dot matrix is an example of using simple analog electronics as a powerful tool in the field of advertising and design.

  • This project can be used to automatically turn off/on the streetlights in accordance with sunrise/sunset. Hence, this project can help us saving power and contribute in solving a major problem.

  • This project can work as a simple camera model(LDRs matrix as the camera and LEDs matrix as the screen).


Improvements Possible:


  • This prototype can be easily modified to be used as a burgulary alarm by replacing LDRs with Infrared Sensors(The whole setup will be responsive towards the infrared radiations coming out of burgulars body and instead of LEDs , alarms are to be used).

  • This prototype can be modified into automated light system.That is the lights that automatically turn on when the person is in the room and turn off when no one is in the room.( The whole setup will be responsive towards the infrared radiations coming out of persons’ body and will turn on or off according to that.).

  • This prototype can be efficiently used to design a light intensity meter.

  • In future, it can have further medical application like inventing new techniques for imaging some internal organs.


Future Prospects:


Even though there is an increasing realisation and recognition that energy conservation is an urgent need of the present day world, that specified problem has not made a big turn over to the situation in which a large amount of energy is being wasted all around the world. In such a voyage of the present-day world, it is no longer in doubt that a situation of severe energy crisis is not that far.

Here comes the relevance of numerous applications of our project such as AUTOMATED STREET LIGHTS, AUTOMATED HOUSEHOLD LIGHTS AND APPLIANCES etc..
